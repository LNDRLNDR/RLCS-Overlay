Rocket League Championship Series Overlay and Game HUD

What is Rocket League Championship Series (RLCS)?

The Rocket League Championship Series also known as RLCS, is the competative esports league for the video game Rocket League. The RLCS represents the highest level of Rocket League esports and attracts the best teams from around the world.

For this Championship Series teams have the chance to broadcast their matches to their fanbase and viewers. To make their broadcasts special and match their team's style, they can use a Custom Dynamic Broadcasting Overlay made by me (LNDRLNDRLNDR).

This overlay changes the usual look of Rocket League to match the team's own colors and design. This enriches the viewer's experience by incorporating additional features, such as a mini-map and team boost meters, seamlessly integrated with the game data to provide a more immersive and detailed perspective.
